A Bit about Myself.

As a Person

You already know that I'm a designer, but I do much more than that. Before I decided that design was the thing I loved to do and could actually make a career of it, I was going to school for music. I've been heavily involved in music for over 13 years and still play in a band, which you can listen to here if you wish.

I am also a very active person. I love the outdoors and do things that get me away from the computer. I love to snowboard, mountain bike, but I mostly love to race motocross. You can catch me out at the track at least once a week as long as life isn't occupying my time with other things.

Education

I spent two years at North Idaho College studying music and classical guitar before transfering to Eastern Washington University. This is where I graduated from with a BA in Visual Communications and Design and a minor in music.
