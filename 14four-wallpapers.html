14four Pax-Dev Wallpapers

Role: Art Direction / Design

14four will be speaking on a panel at Pax-Dev in Seattle in August talking about HTML games as advertising. These mobile wallpapers were designed to be digital prizes the audience can win for completing a VR game 14four has developed for Google Cardboard. Not only are they a cool piece for someone to set their phone wallpaper too, but it's also a small advertisement for 14four in itself. The deer and robot were first hand sketched, then vectorized in Illustrator.
