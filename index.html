Lets make something awesome

Hey, my name is Jim, I am a designer based out of Spokane, Washington. I like doing work in both print and web design/development. If you want to make something awesome with me, drop me a line.

Design

I've worked with many different clients around the Spokane area with many design needs. I like to especially focus on logo and identity designs, but that's just the starting point. After considering your audience, business goals, and personality you want facing the community, we can then develop and iterate on the perfect logo for your company. Or maybe you just need graphics made up for your business's vehicle wrap, or promotional flyers, I've got you covered.

web design

Maybe your old site is dated, broken, embarassing, or you want it to be up to par with your new branding. Either way, together we can build you a new site from the ground up. Not only can I design a site that is aesthetically pleasing, but I like to spend a lot of time on the UX portion and organizing all your content so no information is unreachable. I strongly believe that all web designers should also know how to develop, this guarantees that the layout and behavior of the site will be easy to implement in development.

web dev

Now that we have your new site up to your expectations, I can develop it for you as well. This is the most exciting part of the process as we start to see the final product come alive. After all these pieces have been put together, you can upload, add, and change content as you wish to further personalize your new site.
