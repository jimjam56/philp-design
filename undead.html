Undead Ink Studios Identity

Role: Art Direction/Design

This is the new logo for Undead Ink Studios, a local tattoo parlour. I was given full creative freedom when designing the new identity for Undead Ink. So what goes together better than tattoos and skulls? Nothing. Then again, what is worse than having a skull as a logo? pretty much nothing... So that's why I chose to use a skull as their logo. I wanted to create something awesome that will grow to be recognizable in the tattoo community but also challange myself. I set out to create a logo after one of the most abused images in graphic design (in my personal opinion). I feel this logo turned out great, it goes along with the undead theme, is very dark and grim, while managing to stay classy, professional, and clean.
