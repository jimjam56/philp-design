moto graphics "sketch series"

Role: Art Direction / Designer

This is another personal project done to create a series of MX graphics. I set out to create something unique within the world of MX graphics. The majority of graphics in the industry are created with big, bold lines, geometric shapes, and heavy, bold, sans-serif type. I tried approching from the opposite end with a very messy, sketched collage of characters and a hand-drawn type. Using traditional number typefaces and industry logos, I was able to still give an appearance of a clean, well designed motocycle that was created on the base of chaotic art.
