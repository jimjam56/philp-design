jack's note

Role: Designer

"Jack's Note" is a personal typography piece of mine. The text is from a note that was supposedly left behind by the elusive killer after one of his gruesome attacks. A silhouette was used as the main subject in the text as image piece. It conveys the mysteriousness of Jack as he is surrounded in a world of darkness. As the message is read it becomes almost impossible to read, reflecting the insanity of the killer's mind, and twisted message he was trying to communicate with his actions.
