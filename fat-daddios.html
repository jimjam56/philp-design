Fat Daddio's

Role: Lead Designer / Silver Addy

This project was approached with the intention to give Fat Daddio's a look that was more modern and polished. The old site was very cluttered, difficult to navigate (especially the way products were organized) and pages were designed ununiformed. The new look of the site is bold, while not being over-bearing. The client wanted a good balance between looking to attract national-level clients while maintaining a local business approachability. I decided to add a touch of 50's styling with the logo and other treatments throughout the site, to give it a "small town" feel.
